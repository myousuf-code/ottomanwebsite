Ertugrul's life

Ertugrul ghazi was a great fighter in the olden times.
He was the leader of the Kai tribe. He had 3 sons they were Gunduiz, Sapchi, Osman.
Ertugrul fought many bad guys they were Mongols, saleeby, traitors in seljuk empire and other bad guys.
After his death in 1280 his youngest son Osman became the new leader of the Kai Tribe.
